For more information about the « 3D Slicer for African Scientists » project, please contact Sonia Pujol at spujol@bwh.harvard.edu.
Dr. Pujol is the Director of 3D Slicer Training and Education for Biomedical Research. She has more than 18 years of experience teaching 3D Slicer at academic centers and private institutions around the world.
Sonia Pujol is also an Assistant Professor of Radiology at Harvard Medical School and a clinical neuroscientist at Brigham and Women's Hospital in Boston. She is a member of the Harvard Medical School Academy.
